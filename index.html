Who I Am

I am a postdoctoral fellow working in the group of Prof. Alán Aspuru-Guzik at the Vector Institute for Artificial Intelligence in Toronto (Canada). Previously, I worked in the group of Prof. Bert de Groot at the Max Planck Institute for Biophysical Chemistry in Göttingen (Germany), after having obtained my PhD at the University of Oxford (UK) with Prof. Phil Biggin.

Next

Research

I am broadly interested in the use of computation in the design of organic and biological molecules for biomedical and nanotechnological applications. Currently, I work on the development and application of machine-learning approaches to autonomous experimentation and materials discovery. I am also interested in the use of molecular dynamics simulations and free energy calculations in drug and protein design.
