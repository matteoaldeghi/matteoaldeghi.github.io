Peer Review

I am always happy to receive requests to peer review manuscripts that use computation for research in chemistry and drug discovery — especially those related to machine learning, molecular modelling, cheminformatics, or biomolecular simulations — even though I am able to accept only a limited number.


I have been acting as peer reviewer for both general and specialized journals, including Chemical Science, Nature Machine Intelligence, PNAS, JACS, Angewandte Chemie, Briefings in Bioinformatics, JCIM, and JCTC.
